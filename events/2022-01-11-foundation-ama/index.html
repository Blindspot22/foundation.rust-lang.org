The Rust Foundation Ask Me Anything (AMA) - January 2022

Rust Foundation Executive Director Bec Rumbul is joined by board member Nell Shamrell-Harrington in an Ask-Me-Anything (AMA) interactive webinar to share the latest updates of the Foundation and answer community questions. Moderated by Sage Griffin.
